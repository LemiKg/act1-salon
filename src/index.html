Who We Are

We are a local multicultural Beauty Salon operating in Winchester, VA for the last 18 years. We specialize in all hair types and textures, in addition to other services, such as; nails, massage, facials, barbershop, waxing/threading, professional makeup and more. A unique one of a kind one stop shop - equipped with professionals who make sure every customer is satisfied and taken care of at Act 1.
